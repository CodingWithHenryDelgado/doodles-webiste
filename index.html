Meet Me!

Hey, I'm Doodle! I'm a digital illustrator.

I love to make pieces of horror characters in a much more adorable style than they would usually be in! I work in a very pastel or brightly colored color pallet, for a maximum in the cute and creepy!
I was introduced to art when I was very young, being inspired by mangas such as Tokyo Mew Mew and Sailor moon, but I still have a love for the scary when I watch movies such as Scream or Halloween! Much of my style is inspired by these two mediums! I hope you enjoy my portfolio as I have worked hard on it and I hope to hear from you!
